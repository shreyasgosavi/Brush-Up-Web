Text Styling

Text is placed in element's content-box which starts from the starting position (depending upon the writing mode) and flows in inline-direction which continues until the end-of the line, then it breaks into the new line. Text elements act like inline-elements placed adjacent to one-another.

The styling of of fonts is categorized into two types :

  • Text-Style
  • Text-Layout
